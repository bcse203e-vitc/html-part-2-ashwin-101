Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the onion and garlic.
  3. Add the tomato sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

Calories: 400 per serving

Protein: 12g

Carbohydrates: 60g

Fat: 10g
